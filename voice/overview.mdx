Your voice AI agent is a 24/7 receptionist that picks up every call so you never miss a job. Built for home-service businesses, it answers the phone day and night, talks to callers naturally, captures the details you need, and turns conversations into booked work, even when your team is on a roof, under a sink, or asleep.

## What the voice agent does

When a customer calls your business number, the AI receptionist answers immediately and handles the conversation end to end:

- **Answers calls around the clock.** No voicemail, no hold music, no missed leads after hours, on weekends, or during busy stretches when nobody can pick up.
- **Qualifies the caller.** It collects the essentials, such as the caller's name, the service they need, their address, and the urgency, so you arrive prepared.
- **Answers common questions.** Using your [Knowledge Hub](/voice/knowledge-hub), the agent responds to questions about your hours, service area, pricing approach, and the services you offer.
- **Books appointments.** When a caller is ready, the agent can schedule a job directly against your availability so the booking lands on your [calendar](/jobs/calendar) without a callback.
- **Texts back missed calls.** If a call can't be completed or a caller hangs up, the agent can send an automatic follow-up text to keep the lead warm and reopen the conversation.

Every call and text flows into your [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) and creates or updates a contact in your [CRM](/crm/contacts), so the whole conversation history stays in one place.

<Note>
The voice agent uses your own Twilio or RingCentral phone number. Carrier minutes are billed at cost with no markup from Revenue Sol.
</Note>
