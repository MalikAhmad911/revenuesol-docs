## Send a text-to-pay invoice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the invoice">
    From a job or contact, create an invoice with the line items and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the payment link">
    Send the customer a secure payment link by text (or email). Each link is unique to that invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer pays by card">
    They open the link and pay by card on a secure checkout page — no app or account needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're notified instantly">
    The payment is confirmed automatically and the invoice is marked paid in the CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Payment links are **single-purpose and secure** — each one only allows paying the specific invoice it was created for. Card details are handled by the payment processor (Stripe or Square), never stored by Revenue Sol.
</Note>

## Quotes & approvals

Send a quote and the customer can approve or decline it from a secure link. When approved, you can convert it straight into an invoice and collect payment.

## Recurring billing

For maintenance plans and repeat work, recurring invoices are generated automatically on a schedule — so steady revenue keeps flowing without manual invoicing each cycle.
