The Revenue Sol mobile app puts your entire operation in your pocket. Whether you are the owner checking on the day's pipeline or a field tech wrapping up a job, you can answer customers, update work, and collect payment without sitting at a desk. The app is available for both Android and iOS and stays in sync with everything you do on the web at [app.revenuesol.com](https://app.revenuesol.com).

## Who it's for

The app serves two main roles, and the experience adapts to what each person needs day to day.

- **Owners and office staff** monitor incoming calls and texts, watch the dispatch board, follow up on leads, and keep an eye on payments and reputation.
- **Field technicians** see their assigned jobs, update status from the field, add notes and photos, and take payment on site.

<Note>
Everyone signs in with the same account they use on the web. What each person can see and do is controlled by their permissions, so techs see their work and owners see the full picture.
</Note>

## What you can do

The app mirrors the core parts of Revenue Sol so you rarely need a laptop in the field.

### Unified inbox

Your [inbox](/inbox/overview) brings calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads into one conversation view. You can read and reply to messages on the go, and the AI Employee can keep covering channels when you are heads-down on a job.

### Calls and texts

Place and receive calls and send texts using your connected Twilio or RingCentral number, so customers always see your business line. Call and message history syncs back to each [contact](/crm/contacts) automatically.

### Jobs and dispatch

Open your schedule, see [jobs](/jobs/overview) assigned to you, and move work along as the day unfolds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your job list">
    Find today's jobs and tap into any one for the address, customer details, and scope of work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update status from the field">
    Mark a job on the way, started, or complete so the office and the dispatch board stay current in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes and details">
    Capture what you found, log notes, and keep a record attached to the job.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Owners and dispatchers see those updates instantly, which keeps routing and scheduling decisions accurate without phone tag.

### Payments

Close out a visit by collecting payment right there. Send a text-to-pay link or take a card payment through Stripe so you get paid before you leave the driveway. See [payments](/payments/overview) for the full flow.

### Push notifications

Turn on push notifications to get alerted the moment something needs you, such as a new lead, an inbound call or text, a job update, or a completed payment.

<Tip>
Allow notifications when the app first asks. If you miss the prompt, you can enable them later in your device's settings under the Revenue Sol app.
</Tip>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Install Revenue Sol from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your existing Revenue Sol email and password. Your data is already there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable notifications">
    Accept the notification prompt so you never miss a lead or job update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The mobile app works with your existing account and plan. Set up your phone number, integrations, and team permissions on the web first so the app has everything it needs.
</Warning>
