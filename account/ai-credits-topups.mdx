AI credits power the automated work Revenue Sol does on your behalf — your 24/7 AI receptionist answering and texting back, and the AI Employee that auto-replies across your unified inbox using your Knowledge Hub. Every paid plan includes AI from day one, and each plan comes with a monthly allowance of credits. When you need more in a busy month, you can buy one-time top-up packs that never expire.

## How AI credits work

Each plan includes a monthly AI credit allowance that refreshes at the start of your billing cycle. Credits are consumed as your AI handles work for you — responding to calls and texts, drafting review replies, and answering messages across SMS, email, and web-form leads.

<Note>
AI credits are separate from carrier costs. Calls and texts that flow through your own Twilio or RingCentral number are billed at carrier cost with no markup. Credits cover the AI work, not the phone minutes or message fees.
</Note>

Higher plans include larger monthly allowances. If you consistently use more than your plan provides, you can either upgrade your plan or buy top-up packs to cover the overflow — whichever fits your needs better.

## Monitor your usage

Keep an eye on your balance so the AI never goes quiet mid-conversation with a customer.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open your billing area">
Sign in at [app.revenuesol.com](https://app.revenuesol.com) and go to your account or billing settings.
</Step>
<Step title="Find your credit balance">
You'll see your current AI credit balance, your monthly allowance, and how much you've used in the current cycle.
</Step>
<Step title="Review what's consuming credits">
Check the usage breakdown to understand which activities — receptionist conversations, AI Employee replies, review responses — are drawing down your balance.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Check your balance early in the month and again partway through. If you're trending toward your limit before your cycle resets, top up ahead of time so there's no interruption.
</Tip>

## Buy a top-up pack

Top-up packs are one-time purchases that add credits on top of your monthly allowance. They start at **$20 for 1,000 credits**, and they **never expire** — unused top-up credits carry forward indefinitely, even across billing cycles.

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to AI credits in billing">
From your billing settings, open the AI credits section.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose a top-up pack">
Select the pack size that matches your needs. The $20 / 1,000-credit pack is the entry option, with larger packs available.
</Step>
<Step title="Confirm your purchase">
Pay with your card on file. Credits are added to your balance right away so your AI keeps working without a pause.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Top-up credits are added to — not deducted from — your monthly allowance. Your plan's included credits are used first each cycle, and top-up credits cover anything beyond that. Because top-ups never expire, anything you don't use stays in your account.
</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Do my monthly plan credits roll over?">
Your plan's included monthly allowance refreshes each billing cycle and does not carry over. Only one-time top-up credits never expire.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
Your AI features may pause until your allowance refreshes or you add a top-up pack. To avoid any gap, monitor your usage and top up before you hit zero.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Should I top up or upgrade my plan?">
If you regularly exceed your allowance, upgrading to a higher plan (Professional, Scale, or Enterprise) usually gives you more credits for less. Top-ups are best for occasional busy months.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
