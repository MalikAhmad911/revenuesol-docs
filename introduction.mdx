Revenue Sol is an all-in-one, AI-powered CRM built for **US local service businesses** — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, cleaning, landscaping, pest control, and more. It brings your calls, texts, email, web leads, scheduling, quotes, invoicing, payments, and reviews into one platform, with an AI assistant that answers leads and follows up automatically.

<Note>
**Who this is for:** business owners, office staff, and field crews who want to capture more leads, respond faster, and get paid sooner — without juggling five separate tools.
</Note>

## How Revenue Sol fits together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Leads come in">
    Customers call, text, fill out a form, web-chat, or message you on WhatsApp/Instagram. Everything lands in your **unified inbox**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI responds instantly">
    The **AI receptionist** answers calls and the **AI employee** replies to messages — qualifying leads and booking jobs 24/7.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You do the work">
    Jobs are scheduled and dispatched to your crew, with reminders that cut no-shows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You get paid & reviewed">
    Send a text-to-pay invoice, then automatically request a Google review when the job is done.
  </Step>
</Steps>
