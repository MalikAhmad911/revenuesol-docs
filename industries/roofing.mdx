Roofing is a high-ticket, fast-moving business: leads come in from storm-season ad blitzes, every estimate is competitive, and the company that responds first usually wins the job. Revenue Sol gives roofing contractors one place to capture those leads, quote them, follow up automatically, and turn finished jobs into five-star reviews. It is built by Infinite Rankers LLC for US local home-service businesses, with AI included on every plan.

## Capture every roofing lead

Storm and replacement leads arrive across many channels, and missing one means handing it to a competitor. Revenue Sol pulls them all into a single [unified inbox](/inbox/overview).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your lead sources">
    Route web-form submissions, Facebook and Instagram lead ads (via the [Meta integration](/integrations/meta)), and phone calls and texts from your own Twilio or RingCentral number into one inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer 24/7">
    The [AI receptionist](/ai/receptionist) answers calls and texts after hours and during storm surges, qualifies the homeowner, and books an inspection so no lead goes cold.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize in the CRM">
    New leads land in your [pipeline](/crm/pipelines) with the right [lead stage](/crm/leads), tags, and source attribution so you always know where each roof job stands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Speed wins roofing jobs. Pair lead capture with an automation that texts the homeowner within seconds of an ad or form submission.
</Tip>

## Estimates customers can approve and e-sign

Roofing quotes are detailed (tear-off, materials, square count, warranties), so they need to look professional and be easy to accept. Build them from your [products and pricing catalog](/quotes/products) and send a branded [estimate](/quotes/estimates) the homeowner can review on their phone, approve, and e-sign without printing anything.

<Info>
Approved estimates flow straight into a [job](/jobs/overview) and can convert to an [invoice](/payments/invoices) with text-to-pay, so you collect deposits and final payments fast.
</Info>

## Automate the follow-up

Most roofing deals are not won on the first contact. The [automations engine](/marketing/automations) keeps prospects warm without manual effort:

- Trigger an instant text and email the moment a lead comes in.
- Send timed reminders while an estimate sits unsigned.
- Re-engage cold storm leads weeks later with a seasonal offer.
- Notify your crew and schedule inspections on the [dispatch board](/jobs/dispatch).

You can also run [SMS and email campaigns](/marketing/campaigns) with A/B testing for neighborhood canvassing after a storm rolls through a service area.

## Turn finished roofs into reviews

A strong Google presence drives the next wave of roofing leads. After a job closes, Revenue Sol automatically sends a [review request](/reputation/reviews) and uses AI to draft replies you can approve, so your reputation grows on autopilot through the [Google Business Profile integration](/integrations/google).
