Stripe is how Revenue Sol processes card payments, so you can get paid faster without chasing checks or invoices. Once connected, you can collect card payments, send text-to-pay links, and run recurring billing directly from your CRM. Card-processing fees are billed by Stripe at their standard rates.

## Connect your Stripe account

You connect Stripe once, and every payment surface in Revenue Sol starts working. If you don't already have a Stripe account, you can create one during the connection flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your integration settings">
    Go to your workspace settings and find the Integrations or Payments area, then choose Stripe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Select Connect Stripe. You'll be redirected to Stripe to either sign in to an existing account or create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe's onboarding">
    Stripe asks for your business details, bank account, and tax information so it can verify you and enable payouts. This is required before you can accept live payments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Revenue Sol">
    After approval, you're redirected back and Stripe shows as connected. You're ready to collect payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Revenue Sol never sees or stores your customers' card numbers. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe on its secure, PCI-compliant checkout.
</Note>

## Accept card payments

With Stripe connected, customers can pay invoices and quotes by card on the secure, Stripe-hosted checkout page that Revenue Sol generates. You can collect a payment in a few ways:

- **Payment links** you send by text or email that open a hosted checkout page.
- **Text-to-pay**, where the customer receives an SMS with a tappable link and pays from their phone in seconds.
- **Manual card entry** for taking a card over the phone or in person.

See [Invoices](/payments/invoices) and [Payments](/payments) for how to send and track each payment.

## Text-to-pay

Text-to-pay is the fastest way to close out a completed job. From an invoice, send the customer a payment link by SMS using the phone number you connected through Twilio or RingCentral. The customer taps the link, enters their card, and the invoice is marked paid automatically. No app download or account is required on their end.

<Tip>
  Text-to-pay works best right after the technician finishes the job, while the customer is still on site. Pair it with an automation so the request goes out the moment a job is marked complete.
</Tip>

## Recurring billing

For maintenance plans, service agreements, or any subscription-style billing, you can set up recurring payments through Stripe. Save a customer's card on file with their consent, set the amount and cadence, and Revenue Sol charges the card automatically on schedule. This is ideal for HVAC tune-up plans, pest-control contracts, and recurring cleaning services.

## Payouts

Stripe deposits your collected funds directly into the bank account you added during onboarding. Payout timing and any holds are controlled by Stripe, not Revenue Sol. To review payout schedules, transfers, and processing fees, open your Stripe Dashboard, where each deposit is itemized against the payments that funded it.

<Warning>
  Disputes, refunds, and chargebacks are handled through Stripe. Refunds you issue in Revenue Sol are sent to Stripe to process and may take several business days to reach the customer.
</Warning>

## Where payments appear

Every transaction stays visible inside Revenue Sol so your records match what's in Stripe:

- Paid, pending, and failed payments show on the related [invoice](/payments/invoices).
- A full transaction history lives in your [Payments](/payments) area.
- Revenue from payments feeds your [reports and analytics](/analytics) so you can track collections over time.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My Stripe account shows as not connected">
    Reopen the Stripe integration and reconnect. If Stripe's onboarding wasn't fully completed, finish the remaining verification steps in your Stripe Dashboard, then return to Revenue Sol.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A payment succeeded in Stripe but the invoice still shows unpaid">
    Allow a moment for the two systems to sync. If the status doesn't update, contact support so we can reconcile the payment to the correct invoice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When will I receive my money?">
    Payout timing is set by Stripe based on your account and region. Check the Payouts section of your Stripe Dashboard for the exact schedule.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
