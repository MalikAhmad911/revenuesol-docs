Revenue Sol gives residential and commercial cleaning businesses one platform to capture leads, fill the calendar with recurring jobs, collect payment automatically, and earn five-star reviews. Built by Infinite Rankers LLC, it pairs a 24/7 AI receptionist with online booking and automated billing so you can keep crews moving instead of chasing paperwork.

## Capture every cleaning request

Most cleaning leads come in as calls, texts, or web-form quote requests, often after hours. Your [AI receptionist](/features/ai-receptionist) answers every call and text around the clock, answers questions about your services, and books the job directly into your calendar. Phone calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads all land in one [unified inbox](/features/inbox) so nothing slips through the cracks.

<Tip>
Train the [Knowledge Hub](/features/ai-employee) on your website and FAQs so the AI quotes accurate details about square-footage pricing, add-ons, and service areas before a human ever picks up.
</Tip>

## Let customers book online

Put a booking link on your website, Google profile, and social bios so customers schedule themselves.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Publish your booking page">
    Set up [online booking](/features/online-booking) with the cleaning services, durations, and availability you offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Route new jobs to crews">
    New bookings drop onto the [dispatch board](/features/jobs) so you can assign cleaners and plan efficient routes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and remind">
    Automated reminders go out before each appointment to cut no-shows and last-minute cancellations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bill recurring cleanings on autopilot

Weekly, biweekly, and monthly clients are the backbone of a cleaning business. Set up [recurring payments](/payments/recurring) once and Revenue Sol charges the saved card on schedule, so you stop sending the same invoice every month.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Text-to-pay & payment links" href="/payments/invoices">
    Send a tap-to-pay link by text right after the job. Card payments run through Stripe with no markup from us.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quotes & estimates" href="/features/quotes">
    Send approvable, e-signable estimates for deep cleans and one-time jobs before you dispatch a crew.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
Payments are processed through Stripe. Connect your account once under integrations to accept cards, set up recurring billing, and issue refunds.
</Note>

## Keep clients and earn reviews

Recurring cleaning revenue depends on retention and reputation.

- After each completed job, Revenue Sol can automatically send a [Google review request](/features/reputation) by text while the work is fresh.
- AI-written reply suggestions help you respond to every review quickly and professionally.
- Use [SMS and email campaigns](/features/marketing) to win back lapsed clients, promote seasonal deep cleans, or upsell add-ons, with A/B testing to find what converts.
- Build [automations](/features/automations) that trigger on events like "job completed" or "no booking in 60 days" to re-engage clients without manual work.

## Run the field from your phone

Cleaners spend their day on the move. The [mobile app](/features/mobile-app) for Android and iOS pushes new bookings, schedule changes, and customer messages to your crew in real time, and lets them mark jobs complete from the doorstep.
