All paid plans start with a **14-day free trial** and no credit card. You explore the full product, then pick the plan that fits when the trial ends.
</Note>

## What's included on every plan

AI is built into Revenue Sol on every tier, not sold as an add-on. Across all plans you get:

- The **24/7 AI receptionist** that answers calls and texts and books jobs around the clock.
- The **AI Employee** that auto-replies across channels, powered by AI credits and your [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub).
- A [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) for calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads.
- Full [CRM](/crm/overview): contacts, leads, pipelines, tasks, notes, and tags.
- [Jobs](/jobs/overview): scheduling, a dispatch board, a calendar, online booking, and reminders.
- [Quotes](/quotes/overview), [invoicing, and payments](/payments/invoices) including text-to-pay.
- [Reputation tools](/reputation/overview): automatic Google review requests and AI-written replies.

## Phone & messaging costs

You bring your own [Twilio or RingCentral](/integrations/phone) number. Calls and texts are billed by your carrier at cost with no markup from Revenue Sol, so your monthly plan price stays predictable regardless of call volume.

<Tip>
Not sure which plan fits? Start the 14-day trial on any plan, watch your usage, then move up or down before billing begins. Changing plans later takes effect on your next billing cycle.
</Tip>
