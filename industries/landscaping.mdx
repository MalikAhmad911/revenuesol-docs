Revenue Sol gives landscaping and lawn-care businesses a single place to capture leads, schedule crews, send quotes, and keep customers coming back through every season. Built by Infinite Rankers LLC for US home-service operators, it pairs a 24/7 AI receptionist with a full CRM so no spring rush call or fall cleanup request slips through.

## Capture every lead, even after hours

Demand for landscaping spikes by season and weather, so missed calls are missed revenue. The [AI receptionist](/features/ai-receptionist) answers calls and texts around the clock, books jobs, and routes questions it can answer using your [Knowledge Hub](/features/knowledge-hub). Calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads land together in the [unified inbox](/features/inbox), so your office sees every mowing inquiry, irrigation repair, and design request in one conversation view.

<Tip>
Connect your [Meta integration](/integrations/meta) to pull Facebook and Instagram lead-ad and message inquiries straight into the same inbox during peak ad season.
</Tip>

## Schedule and route your crews

Once a job is booked, manage it from the [Jobs](/features/jobs) module and the dispatch board.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the job">
    Add the property, service type, and crew notes. Recurring services like weekly mowing or biweekly maintenance can be set to repeat automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign and route">
    Use the dispatch board to assign crews and sequence stops so trucks cover a neighborhood efficiently instead of crisscrossing town.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule on the calendar">
    Drop jobs onto the calendar, offer online booking for new requests, and send automatic reminders so customers know when your crew is arriving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update from the field">
    Crews use the [mobile app](/features/mobile-app) to view their route, log work, and get push notifications for new assignments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quote and get paid

Send [quotes and estimates](/features/quotes) for landscape installs, hardscaping, or seasonal contracts that customers can approve and e-sign from their phone. Build a [products and pricing catalog](/features/products) for common line items like mulch, plant material, or per-visit mowing so estimates go out fast.

When the work is done, [invoice and collect payment](/features/payments) with text-to-pay, card payments, and payment links powered by Stripe. Set up [recurring payments](/features/payments) for maintenance plans so monthly or per-visit billing runs on its own.

## Run seasonal campaigns

Landscaping revenue follows the calendar, and Revenue Sol's [marketing tools](/features/marketing) help you fill the schedule before each season turns.

- Send SMS and email campaigns for spring cleanups, fertilization rounds, leaf removal, snow prep, or holiday lighting.
- A/B test subject lines and offers to see what drives the most bookings.
- Use the [automations engine](/features/automations) with triggers and recipes to re-engage past customers automatically when a season approaches.

<Note>
Segment your contacts with tags so you can target the right list, such as "irrigation customers" before winterization or "maintenance plan" clients for renewals.
</Note>

## Build your reputation

After each completed job, Revenue Sol can automatically send a [Google review request](/features/reputation) and reply to reviews with AI-written responses, helping you stand out for local searches like "lawn care near me." Connect [Google Business Profile](/integrations/google) to monitor and respond to reviews in one place.
