The AI Employee is the assistant that works your inbox around the clock. It replies to messages instantly, qualifies leads, drafts follow-ups, and — when you turn on autopilot — handles routine conversations end to end. It learns your business from your [Knowledge Hub](/guides/knowledge-hub), so its answers reflect your real services, pricing, and policies.

## Set up auto-reply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your knowledge base">
    Make sure your [Knowledge Hub](/guides/knowledge-hub) has your services, pricing, hours, and FAQs. This is what the AI uses to answer accurately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the AI Employee settings">
    Go to **AI Employee** in the app and review the assistant's tone and instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a mode">
    - **Suggest** — the AI drafts replies and you approve them.
    - **Autopilot** — the AI replies automatically to routine conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Save your settings. New inbound messages will now be handled according to your chosen mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Autopilot mode

Autopilot lets the AI reply on its own for routine conversations — perfect for after-hours and overflow. You stay in control:

- Conversations remain visible in your [unified inbox](/guides/inbox) in real time.
- You can jump in and take over any conversation at any moment.
- Sensitive or complex situations can be escalated to your team.

<Warning>
Start in **Suggest** mode for a few days to see how the AI handles your real conversations, then switch on **Autopilot** once you're confident in its replies.
</Warning>

## AI memory

Revenue Sol keeps an AI memory for each customer — a running summary of their conversations, preferences, and history (past jobs, payments, and more). This means the AI doesn't re-ask questions a customer already answered and its replies stay consistent over time.

<Tip>
The better your [Knowledge Hub](/guides/knowledge-hub), the better every AI reply. Keep your services and pricing up to date and re-crawl your website after major changes.
</Tip>
