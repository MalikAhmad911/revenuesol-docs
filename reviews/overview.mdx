Your online reputation is often the first thing a customer sees before they ever call. Revenue Sol turns reviews into a steady, automatic flywheel: it asks happy customers for a review right after the job, replies to incoming reviews with AI, and watches your ratings so you never miss a comment. The result is more five-star reviews, faster responses, and stronger local search visibility, all without manual chasing.

## Why reviews drive local search

For home-service businesses, your Google Business Profile ranking and your number of recent reviews directly affect how often you show up in the local map pack and in searches like "plumber near me." Search engines weigh review volume, ratings, and how recently reviews were posted, along with whether you respond. A consistent flow of fresh, positive reviews with thoughtful replies signals an active, trusted business, which helps you outrank competitors and win more calls.

## How automatic review requests work

When a job is completed, Revenue Sol can automatically send the customer a review request by SMS or email with a direct link to leave a Google review. Because the ask goes out promptly while the experience is fresh, response rates are far higher than asking days later or not at all.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Google Business Profile">
    Link your profile so requests point customers to the right place and so reviews flow back into Revenue Sol. See [Connect Google Business Profile](/reviews/google-business-profile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark jobs complete">
    Closing out a job is the trigger. When a job reaches its completed state, the request can fire automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The customer gets the ask">
    Your customer receives a short, branded message with a one-tap link to write a review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reviews and ratings sync back">
    New reviews are pulled into Revenue Sol so you can read, reply, and track them in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Sending the request right after the job is the single biggest lever for review volume. Keep the message short and make the link the obvious next step.
</Tip>

## AI-written review replies

Responding to every review is good for both customers and search ranking, but it is tedious. Revenue Sol drafts AI replies that match the tone of the review, thank the customer by name, and stay on-brand. You can review and edit a suggested response before it posts, or let trusted replies go out automatically, so even a flood of new reviews never sits unanswered.

## Review monitoring

Revenue Sol keeps an eye on your incoming reviews so you always know your current standing. You can see new reviews as they arrive, track your overall rating over time, and act quickly on anything that needs a personal touch.

<Note>
A timely, professional reply to a less-than-perfect review often does more for your reputation than the review itself. Prospective customers read how you handle feedback.
</Note>

## Getting the most out of reputation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When should review requests go out?">
    Right after a job is marked complete. Promptness drives response rates, so avoid long delays between the work and the ask.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to approve every AI reply?">

  <Accordion title="Where do reviews show up?">
