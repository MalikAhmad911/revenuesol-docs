Every missed call is a missed job. When a customer calls your HVAC, plumbing, or roofing business and reaches voicemail, they usually hang up and dial the next company on their list. The Revenue Sol AI receptionist makes sure that never happens. It answers every call and text around the clock, captures the details, and books work straight onto your calendar, so you keep the lead instead of losing it to a competitor.

## How the always-on receptionist works

The AI receptionist picks up calls that you and your team can't get to. It runs on the phone number you connect (your own Twilio or RingCentral number) and answers in a natural voice using Revenue Sol's Knowledge Hub, which is trained on your website, service area, hours, and FAQs.

It handles two situations that quietly drain revenue:

- **After-hours calls.** Evenings, weekends, and holidays are when many home-service emergencies happen. Instead of voicemail, callers reach a receptionist that can answer questions and schedule them.
- **Overflow calls.** When every line is busy or your office staff is on another call, the AI picks up the overflow so no caller waits on hold or gives up.

<Note>
The AI receptionist works over voice and text. Spoken calls and inbound SMS both land in your [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) as one conversation per customer, so nothing slips through the cracks.
</Note>

## Booking jobs onto your calendar

The receptionist does more than take a message. During the conversation it can collect the caller's name, address, and the problem they're describing, then check your availability and place the job on your calendar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="The caller is greeted">
    The AI answers in your business's voice and confirms what the customer needs help with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Details are captured">
    Contact information, service address, and a description of the job are recorded against a new or existing contact in your CRM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A time is scheduled">
    The receptionist offers open slots and books the appointment, which appears on your [jobs calendar](/jobs/calendar) for dispatch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Everyone is notified">
    You get a notification, and the customer receives a confirmation so they know they're on the schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Keep your Knowledge Hub and service hours current. The more accurate your business details, the better the AI answers questions and books the right kind of work.
</Tip>

## Handing off when needed

The AI knows its limits. For situations that need a human, such as a complex quote, a sensitive complaint, or a request outside what the receptionist can resolve, it captures the full context and hands off to your team. Depending on how you configure it, that can mean transferring the live call, flagging the conversation in your inbox, or creating a follow-up task. Because every interaction is logged, whoever picks it up sees the entire history and never asks the customer to repeat themselves.

## The business impact

The payoff is simple: more captured leads and fewer that walk. Calls that used to hit voicemail at 9 p.m. become booked jobs by morning. Overflow during your busy season turns into scheduled work instead of lost opportunities. Since AI is included on every Revenue Sol plan and you bring your own phone number (calls billed at carrier cost, no markup), the receptionist pays for itself the first time it books a job you would otherwise have missed.
