Pest control runs on routine. Quarterly treatments, monthly mosquito service, termite renewals, and seasonal rodent visits all depend on getting the right tech to the right property on schedule, and on keeping customers happy enough to renew. Revenue Sol, built by Infinite Rankers LLC, gives your pest control business one system to capture leads, run recurring service plans, dispatch field crews, and grow your reputation.

## Capture every call and lead

When a homeowner spots ants or a property manager calls about a wasp nest, you cannot afford to miss the call. The [24/7 AI receptionist](/features/ai-receptionist) answers calls and texts around the clock, qualifies the request, and books the appointment even after hours or while your crews are on routes. Calls, texts, emails, and web-form leads all land in one [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) so nothing slips through.

<Tip>
You bring your own Twilio or RingCentral number, so calls and texts are billed at carrier cost with no markup from Revenue Sol.
</Tip>

## Run recurring service plans

Most pest control revenue is recurring, and Revenue Sol is designed around that. Set customers up on repeating visits and let automated [scheduling](/jobs/scheduling) keep the cadence on track.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define the plan">
    Create the recurring service (quarterly general pest, monthly mosquito, annual termite renewal) and link it to your [products and pricing catalog](/quotes/products).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule repeating visits">
    Set the visit interval so jobs are generated automatically on the [calendar](/jobs/calendar) without manual re-entry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill on a recurring cycle">
    Collect with [recurring payments](/payments/recurring) or send a payment link, and let customers settle with text-to-pay or card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dispatch and route the field

Use the [dispatch board](/jobs/dispatch) to assign visits to the right tech and the day's [routing](/jobs/routing) to keep drive time down across spread-out properties. Field techs work from the [mobile app](/features/mobile-app) for Android and iOS, with push notifications, schedule updates, and the ability to log job details from the property.

## Reduce no-shows with reminders

Reliable scheduling depends on customers being ready when your tech arrives. Automatic [appointment reminders](/jobs/reminders) confirm upcoming visits by text and email, which cuts no-shows and locked-gate callbacks on recurring routes.

<Note>
Reminders work alongside [automations](/marketing/automations) so you can trigger pre-visit prep messages, post-treatment follow-ups, or renewal notices without manual work.
</Note>

## Win reviews and referrals

A strong local reputation drives new pest control work. After a completed job, Revenue Sol automatically sends a [Google review request](/reputation/review-requests), and AI can draft replies to incoming reviews so your profile stays active. Monitor all of it from [reputation management](/reputation/overview).

## Quote bigger jobs

For termite jobs, exclusion work, or commercial accounts, send a professional [quote or estimate](/quotes/estimates) that the customer can approve and e-sign online. Approved quotes flow straight into jobs and invoicing.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I bill different cadences for different customers?">
    Yes. Each customer can be on their own recurring schedule and billing cycle, so quarterly, monthly, and annual plans coexist.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a customer wants to skip or reschedule a visit?">
    Adjust the individual occurrence from the calendar or dispatch board without changing the rest of the plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
