Quick answers to the questions you're most likely to have about Revenue Sol, the all-in-one AI CRM built for US local home-service businesses by Infinite Rankers LLC. If you don't find what you need below, reach out and we'll help.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the free trial work?">
    Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial, and you don't need a credit card to begin. During the trial you get full access to the platform, including the AI features that are included on every plan. You can explore the unified inbox, set up your pipelines, connect your phone number, and test the AI receptionist before you commit. When the trial ends, you choose a plan that fits your business; nothing is charged automatically without your card on file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are AI credits and how are they used?">
    AI credits power your AI Employee, the assistant that auto-replies across calls, texts, email, and web-form leads using your Knowledge Hub. Each AI-handled interaction draws from your credit balance. AI is included on every plan, so you start with credits as part of your subscription. To get the most accurate replies, train the Knowledge Hub on your website and FAQs so the AI answers in your business's voice. See [AI credits](/ai/credits) and the [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I bring my own phone number — what do calls and texts cost?">
    You connect your own Twilio or RingCentral number, and calls and texts are billed at carrier cost with no markup from Revenue Sol. You pay your Twilio or RingCentral account directly for usage, and your Revenue Sol subscription covers the CRM, AI, and automation features on top. This keeps your communication costs transparent and predictable.

    <Tip>If you don't have a number yet, set one up with Twilio or RingCentral first, then connect it during onboarding.</Tip>

    Learn more in [phone setup](/setup/phone-numbers).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch from another CRM or scheduling tool?">
    Yes. Many businesses move to Revenue Sol from another CRM, scheduling app, or invoicing tool. You can import your existing contacts and leads, rebuild your pipelines and lead stages, and connect the integrations you already use, such as Stripe, QuickBooks, and Google. The goal is to consolidate calls, texts, email, jobs, quotes, invoices, and reviews into one place so you can retire the patchwork of separate tools.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Export your data">Export contacts and leads from your old tool as a CSV.</Step>
      <Step title="Import into Revenue Sol">Bring the file in through the contacts import. See [data import](/crm/import).</Step>
      <Step title="Connect integrations">Link Stripe, QuickBooks, Google, and your phone provider.</Step>
      <Step title="Rebuild your workflow">Recreate your pipelines, stages, and automations.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    You can cancel from your account's billing settings at any time. Your plan stays active through the end of your current billing period, and you keep access until then. Before you cancel, you may want to export your contacts, leads, and other records so you keep a copy of your data.

    <Note>Need help winding down or have questions about your final invoice? Email <a href="mailto:contact@infiniterankers.io">contact@infiniterankers.io</a> and we'll assist.</Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I import my existing data?">
    Use the contacts import to upload a CSV of your contacts and leads. Map your spreadsheet columns to the matching Revenue Sol fields, such as name, phone, email, and tags, then confirm the import. After importing, organize records with tags and segments and assign them to the right pipeline stages. Full steps and field guidance are in [data import](/crm/import).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
