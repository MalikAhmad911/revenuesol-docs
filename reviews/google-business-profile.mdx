Connecting your Google Business Profile lets Revenue Sol request, monitor, and reply to Google reviews from one place. Once linked, you can send automatic review requests after a completed job, watch new reviews land in real time, and post AI-written replies without leaving the app.

## Why connect Google Business Profile

Google reviews are often the first thing a local homeowner checks before calling an HVAC, plumbing, roofing, or cleaning company. Connecting your profile keeps your reputation work inside Revenue Sol so you do not have to juggle the Google interface separately.

## Before you start

<Note>
You need a verified Google Business Profile and an account that can manage it (an owner or manager). Reviews only appear for a profile that is live and verified on Google.
</Note>

## Connect your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Google integration">
    Go to your integrations or settings area and find the Google connection. See [Google integration](/integrations/google) for the full setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Choose the Google account that manages your Business Profile and approve the access Revenue Sol requests. Use the same login you use on Google Business Profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your business location">
    If your Google account manages more than one location, pick the profile you want to sync reviews for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    Once connected, your existing Google reviews begin syncing into Revenue Sol. The first import can take a few minutes depending on how many reviews you have.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Google reviews access goes through an approval process on Google's side, so review-management permissions are not granted instantly to every account. If review sync or replies are not yet available after you connect, your access may still be in verification. Reach out to contact@infiniterankers.io and the Revenue Sol team will help confirm your status.
</Info>

## After connecting

Once your profile is linked, you can:

- **Automate review requests.** Set jobs to trigger a review request when they are completed so happy customers are prompted to leave a Google review at the right moment.
- **Reply faster.** Use AI-written replies to respond to reviews in your brand voice, then tweak the wording before posting.
- **Stay on top of new reviews.** Monitoring surfaces new reviews so you can respond quickly and protect your rating.

<Tip>
Pair review requests with text-to-pay so customers get a review prompt right after they pay an invoice — when satisfaction is highest.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My reviews are not showing up">
    Confirm your Google Business Profile is verified and live, and that you connected the Google account that actually manages the location. A brand-new connection may also still be importing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I cannot reply to reviews yet">
    Review-reply access depends on Google's approval process. If you are connected but cannot reply, your access may still be in verification — contact contact@infiniterankers.io for help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I manage multiple locations">
    Make sure you selected the correct business location during setup. You can reconnect from the Google integration page to switch locations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
