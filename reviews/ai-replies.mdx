Every Google review deserves a reply, but writing thoughtful responses to each one eats up time you would rather spend in the field. Revenue Sol's AI drafts a tailored reply to every review you receive, matches your tone, and holds it for your approval before anything posts publicly.

## How AI replies work

When a new review lands in your account, the AI Employee reads the rating and the customer's words, then composes a reply that fits the situation. It pulls context from your business so responses feel personal rather than canned.

- **Positive reviews** get a warm thank-you that acknowledges what the customer praised and invites them back.
- **Negative or critical reviews** get a calm, professional response that takes the feedback seriously, apologizes where appropriate, and offers to make things right offline.
- **Neutral or mixed reviews** get a balanced reply that thanks the reviewer and addresses any specific concern.

Replies draw on your [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub), so the AI knows your service area, specialties, and how you talk to customers.

<Note>
AI review replies use AI credits, which are included on every Revenue Sol plan. Drafting a reply costs a small amount of credit per response.
</Note>

## Control the tone

You decide how your replies sound. Set a default tone for your business so the AI stays on-brand across every response.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your reputation settings">
    Go to the Reviews area and open the AI reply settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a tone">
    Choose a voice that matches your brand, such as friendly, professional, or concise. The AI applies it to every draft it writes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add guidance">
    Add notes about phrases to use or avoid, whether to sign off with an owner's name, and how to handle complaints. The AI follows these instructions when drafting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
For negative reviews, ask the AI to invite the customer to contact you directly using your support line. This moves the conversation off the public review and shows future readers you care.
</Tip>

## Approve before posting

By default, nothing posts to Google automatically. Every AI-written reply waits for you to review it, so you stay in full control of your public voice.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get notified">
    When a new review arrives and the AI drafts a reply, you receive a notification, including a push alert on the [mobile app](/getting-started/mobile-app).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the draft">
    Open the review to see the AI's suggested reply alongside the original. Read it in context before deciding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or approve">
    Tweak the wording if you want, then approve to post it to Google. You can also discard a draft and write your own.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Replies post under your connected Google Business Profile and are visible to everyone. Always read negative-review drafts carefully before approving, since tone matters most when a customer is unhappy.
</Warning>

## Connect your reviews first

AI replies only work once your Google Business Profile is connected so Revenue Sol can read incoming reviews and post responses on your behalf. See [Review monitoring](/reviews/monitoring) to connect your profile and start syncing reviews.
