The AI Voice Receptionist answers your business phone with a natural-sounding voice — nights, weekends, and whenever your team is busy. It greets callers, answers common questions, qualifies the job, and books appointments straight onto your calendar. Every call is transcribed and summarized into the [unified inbox](/guides/inbox). Voice AI is powered by Retell.

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect a phone number">
    Make sure you've connected [Twilio](/integrations/twilio) or [RingCentral](/integrations/ringcentral) and chosen a default number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Voice Agent">
    Go to **Voice Agent** in the app to configure your AI receptionist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Retell API key">
    Provide your Retell API key. It's stored encrypted and used to power the voice agent for your business.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the greeting & behavior">
    Set the greeting, tone, and what the agent should ask. It uses your [Knowledge Hub](/guides/knowledge-hub) to answer questions accurately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Route calls to the agent">
    Send inbound (or after-hours/overflow) calls to the AI receptionist. New calls will be answered, booked, and logged automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Appointment booking by voice

When a caller is ready to book, the agent checks your availability and creates the appointment, then sends a confirmation text. The booking flows into your calendar and the customer's record, just like an appointment booked any other way.

<Note>
Calls handled by the voice agent appear in your [unified inbox](/guides/inbox) with a full transcript and summary, so your team always has context for follow-up.
</Note>

## Tips

<Tip>
Use the AI receptionist for **after-hours and overflow** first — it captures the leads you were losing to voicemail without changing how your team answers during the day.
</Tip>

<Warning>
The quality of the agent's answers depends on your [Knowledge Hub](/guides/knowledge-hub). Add your services, pricing, service area, and FAQs before going live.
</Warning>
