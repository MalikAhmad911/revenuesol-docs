Revenue Sol is an all-in-one AI CRM built for US home-service trades, including residential and commercial electricians. Whether you handle service calls, panel upgrades, EV charger installs, or new-construction wiring, Revenue Sol helps you capture every lead, quote on the spot, dispatch your crew, and collect payment without the back-office drag. It is a product of Infinite Rankers LLC.

## Never miss a service call

Electrical work is often urgent, and the job goes to whoever picks up first. Revenue Sol's [24/7 AI receptionist](/features/ai-receptionist) answers calls and texts day or night using your own Twilio or RingCentral number, so a homeowner with a tripped panel at 9pm still gets a real response and a booked visit.

Every call, text, email, and web-form lead lands in one [unified inbox](/features/inbox), so your office isn't juggling voicemails and sticky notes. The [AI Employee](/features/ai-employee), trained on your services and FAQs through the Knowledge Hub, can auto-reply to common questions like service-area, licensing, and rough pricing.

## Quote on site and win the job

Speed wins electrical jobs. From your phone in the field, build a professional [quote or estimate](/features/quotes) using your saved products and pricing catalog: breakers, fixtures, outlets, labor rates, and common job bundles.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build the estimate">
    Pull line items from your catalog or add custom ones for the panel, wiring, and labor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send for approval">
    Text or email the quote to the customer so they can review it on any device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect the e-signature">
    The customer approves and e-signs, locking in the scope before you order materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Save recurring jobs like outlet installs or EV charger setups as reusable quote templates to cut your estimating time to seconds.
</Tip>

## Schedule and dispatch your crew

Once a quote is approved, turn it into a [job](/features/jobs) and place it on the [dispatch board](/features/dispatch). Route technicians efficiently, view the team calendar, and let customers self-book through [online booking](/features/online-booking). Automatic reminders cut no-shows so your billable hours stay full.

Your electricians work the schedule from the [mobile app](/features/mobile-app) on Android or iOS, getting push notifications, job details, and the ability to update status from the truck.

## Invoice and get paid faster

Skip the wait for a mailed check. Revenue Sol [invoicing and payments](/features/payments) supports text-to-pay, card payments through Stripe, payment links, and recurring billing for maintenance agreements with commercial clients.

<Note>
Phone numbers are bring-your-own (Twilio or RingCentral), billed at carrier cost with no markup. Card processing runs through Stripe.
</Note>

## Follow up and earn 5-star reviews

After a job closes, Revenue Sol automatically sends a [Google review request](/features/reputation) and can draft AI-written replies to the reviews you receive. Use [marketing campaigns](/features/marketing) and the automations engine to send seasonal reminders, like annual safety inspections or generator checks, that bring past customers back.
