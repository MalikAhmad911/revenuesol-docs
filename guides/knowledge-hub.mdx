The Knowledge Hub is where you teach Revenue Sol's AI about your business. Everything here powers the [AI Employee](/guides/ai-employee) and the [AI Voice Receptionist](/guides/voice-agent) — so their answers reflect your actual services, pricing, hours, service area, and policies instead of generic guesses.

## Import your website

The fastest way to fill the Knowledge Hub is to let Revenue Sol crawl your existing website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Knowledge Hub">
    Go to **AI Brain → Knowledge** (Knowledge Hub) in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a website import">
    Enter your website URL and start the crawl. Revenue Sol reads your pages and extracts the content into your knowledge base.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review what was imported">
    The crawl runs in the background. When it finishes, review the imported content and edit anything that's out of date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add anything missing">
    Manually add services, pricing, FAQs, and policies the AI should know but that aren't on your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The website import runs as a background job, so you can keep working while it processes. Larger sites take longer to crawl.
</Note>

## Keep it fresh

Your AI is only as accurate as your Knowledge Hub. Whenever your services, pricing, or policies change:

- Update the relevant entries directly, or
- Re-run the website import after you publish changes to your site.

<Tip>
A well-maintained Knowledge Hub is the single biggest lever for great AI replies. Five minutes keeping it current pays off in every conversation the AI handles.
</Tip>
