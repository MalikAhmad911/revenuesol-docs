Revenue Sol is the all-in-one AI CRM built for US local home-service businesses. It brings your phone, texts, email, web leads, scheduling, quotes, invoicing, and reviews into a single platform so nothing slips through the cracks, even when you're on a job site. Revenue Sol is a product of Infinite Rankers LLC, and our team is here to help at [contact@infiniterankers.io](mailto:contact@infiniterankers.io).

## Who it's for

Revenue Sol is designed for service trades that win or lose work by how fast they respond and how smoothly they get jobs booked and paid:

- HVAC, plumbing, and electrical
- Roofing and general contracting
- Cleaning, landscaping, and pest control

If you're a small-to-midsize crew juggling missed calls, scattered text threads, and manual invoicing, Revenue Sol replaces a stack of disconnected tools with one system.

## How it fits together

A lead comes in through a call, text, web form, or social message. The AI receptionist and AI Employee respond instantly, capture the details, and create a contact in your CRM. From there you move the lead through your pipeline, schedule a job on the dispatch board, send a quote for e-signature, and collect payment by text-to-pay. After the job, Revenue Sol automatically requests a Google review. Every touchpoint stays linked to the same customer record, so you always have the full history in one place.
