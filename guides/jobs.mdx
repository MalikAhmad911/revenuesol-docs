## Create and schedule a job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the job">
    From a contact or the Jobs area, create a job and add the details — service, address, notes, and schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a crew">
    Assign the job to a team member or crew. They'll see it on their schedule and in the [mobile app](/guides/mobile-app).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with the customer">
    Send an automatic confirmation and reminder by text to cut no-shows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track to completion">
    Update the job status as work progresses, then turn it into an [invoice](/guides/payments) and request a [review](/guides/reviews).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dispatch & routing

The dispatch board gives the office a single view of the day's work across the team — what's scheduled, who's assigned, and what's still open. The route view lays jobs out by location so you can sequence them efficiently and reduce drive time.

## Recurring jobs

For maintenance plans and repeat work, Revenue Sol can generate recurring jobs automatically on a schedule, so you don't have to recreate them each cycle.

<Note>
Recurring jobs and recurring invoices are generated by scheduled background tasks, so they appear on time without manual effort.
</Note>
