This guide walks a new business owner from sign-up to a working setup that captures and responds to leads. Total time: about 5 minutes.

<Note>
You sign in to Revenue Sol at [app.revenuesol.com](https://app.revenuesol.com). Want to look around first? Try the [live demo](https://demo.revenuesol.com) — no account needed.
</Note>

## 1. Create your account & business

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [app.revenuesol.com](https://app.revenuesol.com) and create your account. Authentication is handled securely by Clerk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    A short wizard sets up your workspace:
    - **Industry** — pick your trade (HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, etc.)
    - **Business basics** — name, phone, website, and city/state
    - **Your goal** — reply faster, get more reviews, book more jobs, or automate follow-ups
    - **AI sync** — Revenue Sol crawls your website to build your AI knowledge base
    - **Ready** — you land in your dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Adding your **website** during onboarding lets the AI learn your services, pricing, and FAQs automatically — so its replies sound like your business from day one. See the [Knowledge Hub guide](/guides/knowledge-hub).
</Tip>

## 2. Connect a phone number

This is the most important step — it lets Revenue Sol send and receive calls and texts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Connectors">
    Go to **Settings → Connectors** in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Twilio or RingCentral">
    Choose your provider and follow the secure connect flow. You bring your own account, so calls and texts are billed at carrier cost.
    - [Connect Twilio →](/integrations/twilio)
    - [Connect RingCentral →](/integrations/ringcentral)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a default number">
    Select the phone number Revenue Sol should use for messaging and calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Set up how you get paid & reviewed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Payments">
    Enable [payments](/guides/payments) so you can send invoices and text-to-pay links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reviews">
    Turn on [automatic review requests](/guides/reviews) so every completed job grows your Google rating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 5. Invite your team

Go to **Settings → Team** to invite office staff and field crew. Roles control who can see and do what. Field crews can also use the [mobile app](/guides/mobile-app).

## You're live 🎉

Your business is now set up to capture leads, respond automatically, schedule jobs, get paid, and grow reviews — all from one platform.
