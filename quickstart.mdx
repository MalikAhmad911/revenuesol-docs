This guide takes you from a brand-new account to your first AI-booked job in roughly 15 minutes. Revenue Sol is the all-in-one AI CRM for home-service businesses from Infinite Rankers LLC, and you can complete every step below during your free trial.

<Info>
Revenue Sol offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required, and AI is included on every plan. You can test the full experience before you decide.
</Info>

## Before you start

Have these handy to keep things moving:

- Your business name, service area, and a few common services
- Your Twilio or RingCentral account login (you bring your own number)
- A spreadsheet or contact export, if you want to import existing customers
- Your business website URL or a short FAQ to train the AI

## Step-by-step setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account and start the free trial">
    Go to [app.revenuesol.com](https://app.revenuesol.com) and sign up with your work email. No credit card is required to begin the 14-day trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    The onboarding wizard walks you through your business profile, your trade (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, and more), and your service area. These details help the AI answer callers accurately and route work correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your phone number">
    Connect your own Twilio or RingCentral number so calls and texts flow into Revenue Sol. Calls and texts are billed at carrier cost with no markup. See [Connect your phone number](/getting-started/connect-phone) for the full walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your contacts">
    Bring your existing customers in by uploading a CSV. Map the columns to fields like name, phone, email, and tags so your CRM is populated from day one. More detail lives in [Import contacts](/crm/import-contacts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on the AI Employee">
    Enable the AI Employee and point its Knowledge Hub at your website or FAQs. Once trained, it auto-replies across calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads, and books jobs around the clock. The AI runs on AI credits, which are included on every plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a first test message">
    Text your connected number from your own phone with a typical customer question, such as asking about availability or pricing. Watch the conversation appear in your [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) and see the AI Employee reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See a lead book a job">
    Continue the test conversation as if you were a customer. The AI captures the lead, offers available times, and books the appointment onto your calendar and dispatch board, ready for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Test from a phone number that is not your business line so the conversation is treated as a real inbound lead. This shows you exactly what your customers experience.
</Tip>

## Verify it worked

After your test, confirm the basics are in place:

- The conversation shows in your unified inbox under one contact
- A new lead or contact was created in your CRM
- A job appears on your calendar or dispatch board
- The booking confirmation was sent back to the test number
