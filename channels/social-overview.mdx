Your customers reach out where they already spend time: Facebook and Instagram. Revenue Sol connects to Meta so messages and lead-form submissions from those platforms flow straight into your CRM, where you can reply, qualify, and book jobs without juggling separate apps.

## How social leads flow in

Once you connect your Facebook Page and Instagram account, Revenue Sol listens for two kinds of activity:

- **Direct messages.** When someone messages your Facebook Page or your Instagram business profile, the conversation lands in your [unified inbox](/channels/inbox) alongside your calls, texts, and emails.
- **Lead forms.** Submissions from Meta lead ads and instant forms are captured automatically and turned into [leads](/crm/leads) in your pipeline, with the contact details the person provided.

Because everything routes through one inbox and one contact record, you see a complete history for each customer no matter how they first got in touch.

<Info>
Social messaging runs through your Meta connection, not your Twilio or RingCentral phone number. Standard calling and texting still use your own carrier number.
</Info>

## What you can do with social conversations

After a social lead or message arrives, you work it the same way you would any other channel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read and reply in the inbox">
    Open the conversation in the unified inbox and respond directly. Your reply is sent back to the customer on Facebook or Instagram.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let the AI Employee help">
    With your [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub) trained, the AI Employee can auto-reply to common questions and keep prospects engaged after hours, using AI credits included on your plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn it into a lead or job">
    Move qualified conversations into a pipeline stage, add tasks and notes, or create a [job](/jobs/overview) and schedule it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow up across channels">
    Add the contact to an SMS or email campaign, or trigger an automation so no lead goes cold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Meta limits how long businesses can message a person after their last message (a standard messaging window). Reply promptly, or use the AI Employee to keep the conversation moving inside that window.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a Facebook Business Page?">
    Yes. Meta messaging and lead ads run through a Facebook Page, and Instagram messaging requires a professional (business or creator) Instagram account linked to that Page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the AI receptionist answer social messages?">
    The AI Employee handles text-based channels including social messages, using your Knowledge Hub. The 24/7 AI receptionist focuses on voice calls and texts to your phone number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there extra carrier fees for social messages?">
    No. Social messaging goes through Meta, so there are no per-message carrier charges like there are for SMS through your Twilio or RingCentral number.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
