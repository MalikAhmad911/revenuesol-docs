## Turn on automatic requests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Reviews">
    Go to **Reviews** in the app and review your settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger & message">
    Choose when the request is sent (for example, after a job is marked complete) and customize the message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Save. From now on, completed jobs automatically trigger a review request by text.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The best time to ask for a review is right after a great job, while the customer is happy. Automating the request means it always happens — and on time.
</Tip>

## Bulk review requests

Have a backlog of happy customers who never left a review? Send a bulk request campaign to a selected list of contacts to collect reviews in one push.

## Reply with AI

Responding to reviews — positive and negative — signals an engaged business and helps your local ranking. Revenue Sol can draft on-brand replies with AI, so you can respond to every review quickly.

## Embed the review widget

Show your best reviews on your website with an embeddable widget.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your widget snippet">
    In the reviews/widget settings, copy your widget embed code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste it on your site">
    Add the snippet to your website where you want testimonials to appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The public review widget only displays your **highest-rated** reviews and uses a public widget key (no sensitive data is exposed). See [Embeds & widgets](/developers/embeds) for the technical details.
</Note>
