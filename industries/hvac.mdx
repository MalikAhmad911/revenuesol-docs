Revenue Sol is an all-in-one AI CRM built for US home-service businesses, and HVAC shops use it to capture every call, dispatch technicians, bill recurring maintenance plans, and grow their Google reputation. From the first emergency call to the post-job review request, you run the whole operation in one place.

## Never miss an emergency call

A no-heat call at 2 a.m. or a failed AC unit in a July heatwave is revenue you can't afford to miss. Revenue Sol's [24/7 AI receptionist](/ai/receptionist) answers calls and texts around the clock, triages the issue, and books the job straight onto your calendar, even when your team is asleep or already on a job.

Calls, texts, web-form leads, and emails all land in one [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) as a single conversation thread, so dispatchers and office staff always see the full history with a customer.

<Note>
You bring your own Twilio or RingCentral number, and calls and texts are billed at carrier cost with no markup. AI is included on every plan.
</Note>

## Dispatch and schedule your techs

Turn booked calls into jobs and get crews to the door fast.

<Steps>
<Step title="Create the job">
Convert a lead or inbound call into a [job](/jobs/overview) with the service type, address, and customer details attached.
</Step>
<Step title="Assign on the dispatch board">
Use the [dispatch board](/jobs/dispatch) to assign technicians, balance the day's load, and plan efficient routes across service areas.
</Step>
<Step title="Schedule and remind">
Place jobs on the [calendar](/jobs/calendar) and send automatic appointment reminders so customers are home and ready when your tech arrives.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Let customers self-serve with [online booking](/jobs/online-booking) for tune-ups and non-emergency service, which keeps your phone lines open for true emergencies.
</Tip>

## Quote, invoice, and get paid

Build estimates from your [products and pricing catalog](/quotes/catalog) so a replacement system or repair quote takes minutes. Customers can approve and [e-sign quotes](/quotes/overview) from their phone. When the work is done, send an invoice with [text-to-pay](/payments/text-to-pay) and accept card payments through Stripe, including [payment links](/payments/payment-links) and refunds.

## Bill maintenance plans automatically

Seasonal maintenance agreements are steady, predictable revenue. Set up [recurring payments](/payments/recurring) so members are charged automatically each month, quarter, or season without your office chasing renewals. Pair recurring billing with scheduled tune-up jobs and reminders to keep plan members on the books all year.

## Grow your Google reviews

Reputation drives HVAC lead flow. Revenue Sol [automatically requests a Google review](/reputation/review-requests) after each completed job and uses AI to draft on-brand replies for you to approve. [Review monitoring](/reputation/overview) keeps every rating in one view so you can respond quickly and protect your local ranking.

## Market in the slow season

Fill shoulder-season gaps with [SMS and email campaigns](/marketing/campaigns) for filter reminders, tune-up specials, and equipment upgrade offers. The [automations engine](/marketing/automations) uses triggers and recipes to follow up with unsold quotes, win back lapsed customers, and nudge plan renewals on autopilot.
