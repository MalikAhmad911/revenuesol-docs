When your office is closed, every missed call is a missed job. Revenue Sol keeps your phone covered overnight, on weekends, and on holidays so leads never slip away. Whether the AI receptionist answers, a caller leaves a voicemail, or a text comes in, the conversation lands in your unified inbox ready for you to pick up first thing in the morning.

## How after-hours calls are handled

You define your business hours in your settings, and Revenue Sol uses them to decide how to treat each incoming call. Outside those hours, you have a few options for what happens:

- The 24/7 [AI receptionist](/voice/ai-receptionist) answers the call, talks to the caller, answers common questions from your [Knowledge Hub](/ai-employee/knowledge-hub), and can book a job on the spot.
- The call rolls to voicemail so the caller can leave a message.
- The caller receives an automatic text-back inviting them to reply by SMS instead.

<Note>
The AI receptionist works around the clock on every plan, so "after-hours" does not have to mean "no one answers." Many home-service businesses let the AI take overnight calls and only fall back to voicemail for specific scenarios.
</Note>

## Voicemail capture and transcription

When a caller leaves a voicemail, Revenue Sol captures the recording and automatically transcribes it to text. Both the audio and the transcript appear as a new conversation in your [unified inbox](/inbox/overview), attached to the matching contact (or a new lead if the number is unknown).

<Steps>
  <Step title="A caller leaves a message">
    The voicemail is recorded against your Twilio or RingCentral number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The message is transcribed">
    Revenue Sol converts the audio to a readable transcript so you can scan it without listening.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It lands in your inbox">
    The voicemail, transcript, and caller details show up as a conversation, and you get a push notification on the [mobile app](/mobile/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="You follow up">
    Reply by text or call back directly from the conversation, and convert the caller into a lead or job.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Reading the transcript first lets you triage a stack of overnight voicemails in seconds and reply to the hottest leads before your competitors open.
</Tip>

## Automatic text-back so leads aren't lost

A missed call after hours often means a lost customer, because the caller simply dials the next business on their list. To prevent that, Revenue Sol can send an automatic text-back to anyone whose call you miss outside business hours.

The text-back opens a two-way SMS conversation in your inbox, and you can let the [AI Employee](/ai-employee/overview) handle the reply automatically using your AI credits and Knowledge Hub, or jump in yourself. Either way, the lead stays engaged instead of going cold overnight.

<Info>
Texts and calls are sent through your own Twilio or RingCentral number and billed at carrier cost with no markup. Make sure a number is connected so after-hours messaging works. See [Phone setup](/voice/phone-setup).
</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I set my business hours?">
    Business hours live in your account settings. Once configured, they drive whether incoming calls are treated as during-hours or after-hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the AI book jobs overnight?">
    Yes. The AI receptionist can answer, qualify the caller, and book a job at any hour, then add it to your calendar and dispatch board.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a caller texts instead of leaving a voicemail?">
    Inbound texts after hours appear in the same unified inbox and can be answered automatically by the AI Employee or by you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I get notified about overnight messages?">
    Yes. New voicemails and texts trigger push notifications through the mobile app so nothing waits unseen until morning.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
