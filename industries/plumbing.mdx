Plumbing leads do not wait. A burst pipe at 11 p.m. or a backed-up main on a Sunday goes to whoever picks up first. Revenue Sol, an all-in-one AI CRM built by Infinite Rankers LLC, makes sure that company is you — answering, dispatching, collecting payment, and earning the review while the water is still on the floor.

## Never miss an emergency call

Most lost plumbing jobs are simply missed calls. Your [AI receptionist](/features/ai-receptionist) answers every call and text 24/7, qualifies the problem, and books the job straight onto your schedule — even overnight, on weekends, and when your crew is already on a site.

<Note>
You bring your own Twilio or RingCentral number, so calls and texts are billed at carrier cost with no markup. See [phone setup](/setup/phone-numbers).
</Note>

Calls, SMS, email, and web-form leads all land in one [unified inbox](/features/inbox), so a customer who calls and then texts a photo of the leak stays in a single conversation thread your whole team can see.

## Dispatch the closest tech fast

When a call comes in, speed to the door wins the job. Turn the lead into a job and assign it from the [dispatch board](/jobs/dispatch-board), where you can see who is available and route the nearest technician to the address.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture the job">
    The AI receptionist or your office staff creates the [job](/jobs/overview) from the call, with the address, problem, and customer details attached.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign and route">
    Drag the job to an available tech on the dispatch board and let routing order the day's stops efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with the customer">
    Automatic [reminders](/jobs/reminders) text the customer an arrival window so they know help is on the way.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
For non-emergency work like water-heater replacements, share an [online booking](/jobs/online-booking) link so customers self-schedule without tying up your phone.
</Tip>

## Quote and get paid on site

Once the tech diagnoses the issue, build a [quote](/sales/quotes) from your products and pricing catalog that the customer can approve and e-sign on the spot. Then turn it into an [invoice](/payments/invoices) and collect before you leave the driveway.

With [text-to-pay](/payments/text-to-pay), you send a payment link by SMS and the customer pays by card in seconds — no checks, no waiting on a mailed invoice. Recurring payments cover maintenance plans, and refunds are handled in-app.

<Info>
Card payments run through Stripe. Connect your account on the [payments setup](/setup/payments) page.
</Info>

## Turn finished jobs into 5-star reviews

A clean job deserves a public review. Revenue Sol automatically sends a [Google review request](/features/reputation) after the job is marked complete, then uses AI to draft replies to the reviews you receive, so your reputation grows without manual effort.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if the customer only texts, never calls?">
    Inbound texts are handled the same way — the AI Employee can reply instantly across channels using your [Knowledge Hub](/features/ai-employee), and everything stays in the unified inbox.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can my techs update jobs from the field?">
    Yes. The [mobile app](/features/mobile-app) for Android and iOS sends push notifications and lets techs update job status, notes, and photos on the go.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does it work with my existing tools?">
    Revenue Sol integrates with QuickBooks, Stripe, Google, Meta, and your Twilio or RingCentral number. See [integrations](/integrations/overview).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
