## How the free trial works

Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial, and you don't need a credit card to begin. During the trial you get full access to your plan's features, including AI on every tier.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start your trial">
    Sign up at app.revenuesol.com and choose a plan. No card is required to explore Revenue Sol.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a payment method before the trial ends">
    To keep your account active after day 14, add a card in your billing settings. Your subscription begins at your selected plan's monthly rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue or cancel">
    If you add a payment method, your trial converts to a paid subscription automatically when the 14 days end. If you don't, your account pauses rather than charging you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Phone and messaging costs (calls and texts) are billed by your own Twilio or RingCentral account at carrier cost. Revenue Sol does not mark these up. See [Bring your own number](/getting-started/phone-numbers) for details.
</Note>

## Changing your plan

You can move between Starter ($65/mo), Professional ($199/mo), and Scale ($399/mo) at any time from your billing settings. Enterprise pricing is custom.

- **Upgrading** takes effect immediately so you can use the higher tier's limits right away. Charges are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.
- **Downgrading** takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you keep your current features until then.

<Tip>
If you're consistently running low on AI credits, upgrading a tier is usually more cost-effective than buying repeated top-ups.
</Tip>

## AI credits, overages, and top-ups

Your AI Employee and AI receptionist run on AI credits included with your plan. Credits are consumed as the AI replies across channels and handles calls and texts.

- Each plan includes a monthly credit allotment that refreshes at the start of your billing cycle.
- If you use all your included credits, you can purchase a one-time **top-up** to keep the AI running until your next refresh.
- Top-ups are charged to your payment method on file and apply to your current cycle.

<Info>
You can monitor remaining credits in your account so you're never caught off guard. Learn how the AI uses them in [AI credits](/ai/credits) and [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub).
</Info>

## Refunds policy

Card payments and subscriptions are processed securely through Stripe.

<Warning>
Subscription fees are generally non-refundable for the current billing period. Because you can cancel anytime to stop future charges, and the 14-day trial lets you evaluate Revenue Sol before paying, we recommend trying the product fully during your trial.
</Warning>

If you believe you were billed in error, contact <a href="mailto:contact@infiniterankers.io">contact@infiniterankers.io</a> with your account details and we'll review it.

## Updating your payment method

You can update your card whenever it expires or changes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing settings">
    Go to your account's billing area in app.revenuesol.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your card">
    Add or replace the card on file. Your new card is used for the next subscription charge and any top-ups.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will I be charged when my trial ends?">
    Only if you've added a payment method. With a card on file, your trial converts to your chosen paid plan automatically. Without one, your account pauses instead of charging you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do call and text charges come from Revenue Sol?">
    No. You bring your own Twilio or RingCentral number, and calls and texts are billed by that carrier at cost with no markup from Revenue Sol.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of AI credits mid-cycle?">
    Buy a top-up to continue immediately, or your credits refresh at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel?">
    Cancel anytime from your billing settings. You'll keep access through the end of your current paid period, and future charges stop.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
