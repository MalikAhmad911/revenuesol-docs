The AI Employee is your always-on teammate inside Revenue Sol. It reads incoming messages, replies in your business's voice, answers common questions, qualifies the lead, and books appointments, so no opportunity slips through while you're on a job site or asleep. It's included on every plan, from Starter through Enterprise, with AI credits to power its replies.

## What the AI Employee does

Whenever a new message arrives across your connected channels, the AI Employee can step in and handle it for you:

- **Auto-replies to texts and web leads.** It responds to inbound SMS and web-form submissions in seconds, so leads never wait for a callback.
- **Qualifies the lead.** It asks the right follow-up questions, gathers contact details and the nature of the job, and routes the lead into your pipeline.
- **Answers FAQs.** Drawing on your [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub), it answers questions about pricing, service areas, hours, and what you do, using accurate information you've trained it on.
- **Books appointments.** When a lead is ready, it offers available times and books the job directly onto your calendar.

Every reply and booking shows up in your [unified inbox](/inbox/overview) alongside human conversations, so you always have full visibility and can take over at any time.

<Note>
The AI Employee learns from your **Knowledge Hub**, trained on your website, FAQs, and the details you provide. The better it's trained, the more accurate and on-brand its replies. See [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub) to set it up.
</Note>

## How it differs from the voice receptionist

Revenue Sol has two AI surfaces that work together but handle different conversations:

| | AI Employee | AI receptionist (voice) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Channel | Text, web-form leads, and chat | Inbound and after-hours phone calls |
| What it does | Reads and writes messages, qualifies, books | Answers and speaks on live calls, books |
| Best for | SMS and online inquiries | Callers who reach your business number |

The **24/7 AI receptionist** picks up the phone, holds a natural spoken conversation, and books jobs around the clock. The **AI Employee** handles everything that arrives as a message rather than a call. Together they cover every way a customer might reach out. Learn more in [AI receptionist (voice)](/ai/voice-receptionist).

<Tip>
Both AI surfaces book into the same calendar and log every interaction to the lead's record, so a customer who texts first and calls later is treated as one continuous conversation.
</Tip>

## Getting started

<Steps>
<Step title="Train your Knowledge Hub">
Add your website, services, pricing, and FAQs so the AI Employee answers accurately. See [Knowledge Hub](/ai/knowledge-hub).
</Step>
<Step title="Connect your channels">
Make sure your phone number (Twilio or RingCentral) and any web forms are connected so messages flow into your inbox.
</Step>
<Step title="Turn on auto-reply">
Enable the AI Employee for the channels you want it to handle, then watch its replies in your unified inbox.
</Step>
</Steps>
